# Demo Queries

## Example Queries for Multimodal System Demonstration

### Flood Response Policy (Text + Image Retrieval)
"Show me policies similar to successful flood response strategies."

### Cybersecurity Meeting Notes (Text Retrieval)
"Retrieve scanned meeting notes about cybersecurity policies."

### Infrastructure Diagrams (Image Retrieval)
"Find diagrams showing infrastructure upgrade plans and their outcomes."

### Budget Analysis (Text + Image)
"What lessons were learned from the 2020 budget cuts in emergency services?"

### Performance Metrics (Multimodal)
"How have infrastructure upgrades affected system performance and reliability?"

### Emergency Preparedness (Text + Visual)
"What strategies have been successful in improving emergency preparedness?"

### Resource Allocation (Evidence-based)
"How should resources be allocated for disaster recovery based on past experiences?"

### Visual Policy Comparison
"Compare visual representations of policy implementations across departments."

### Historical Timeline (Text + Images)
"Show the timeline of emergency management improvements with supporting documents and images."

### Outcome Analysis (Multimodal)
"What visual evidence exists for the success of recent policy changes?"

## Expected Response Structure
For each query, the system should provide:
1. Retrieved historical memories
2. Analysis of patterns and outcomes
3. Evidence-based recommendations
4. Potential risks and considerations
